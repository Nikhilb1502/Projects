Exploratory Data Analysis on US Accidents Dataset

In this EDA I'm trying to answer the below questions-

  1. Which states has the most number of accidents?
  2. Why does new york doesnt show up in top 10 accident data even when having most population (referred from google)
  3. Among the top 100 cities in number of accidents ,which states do they belong the most frequently
  4. Are there more accidents in warmer or colder areas?
  5. What time of the day are accidents most frequent in?

But before Answering all the questions we should First Clean and prepare our data for Analysis purpose.

Summary And Conclusion¶

Top findings-

  1. Cities like Miami(106966), Los Angeles(68956) and Orlando(54691) has reported most number of accidents.
  2. From 11682 cities 496 cities reported more than 1000 accident cases which is 4.24% of total cities.
  3. 1110 Number of cities reported only 1 accident which should be clarified.
  4. OHIO State reported the most number of accidents which is 64% of total accidents.
  5. Having in mind that new york has the most number of population, we got to know this dataset does not consists any data from New York.
  6. High percent of accidents occured in between 1PM to 5PM.
  7. Most of the accidents took place on workdays, on the other hand weekend have comparatively less amount of accidents.
  8. December month reported most numbers of accidents in last three consecutive year(2019,2020,2021).
  9. Winter is not a factor of incresed accidents in December.
  10. More than 75% of accidents seems to be happend when temperature was in between 40 to 70 Fahrenheit.
  11. 1107194 is the number of accidents happened in fair weather conditions.
  12. There is no co-relation between Timing and Weather Conditions.
